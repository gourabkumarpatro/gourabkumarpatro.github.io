About Me

I am a PhD scholar (TCS research fellow) in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, India. I work under the supervision of Prof. Niloy Ganguly. I also collaborate with Prof. Krishna P. Gummadi and Dr. Abhijnan Chakraborty. My research area is Information Retrieval and Data Mining, and my research interests fall in bias & fairness aspects of AI.

Currently, I am a part of Complex Networks Research Group at IIT Kharagpur. Prior to this, I have completed bachelor of technology from IIT Jodhpur, and also briefly worked as a supply chain and operations planning consultant at Steelwedge Software (now a part of E2open LLC.).
